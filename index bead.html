Our Goal
Every person is a possessor of special and significant memories that they hold dear to themselves. Memories that are redolent of their unique Avocations, Intimate Relationships and departed Confidantes, shaping them into who they are today.
Quotes are nothing less than the flamboyant utility in this arsenary of memories, playing a role of propelling individial opinions and ideas to multiple generations ahead like no other. Quotes are often linked with a pre-experienced plethora of memories, which when we recall them it causes us to relive and reminisce with great Euphoria. These Evocative and Vivid experiences are the reason we must safeguard Quotes with utmost passion. This is our goal.
Sometimes you will never know the value of a moment, until it becomes
a memory.
